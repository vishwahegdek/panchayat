About Our Panchayat

Manjuguni Village Panchayat serves a population of 5,000 residents across 12 hamlets. Established in 1975, our panchayat has been dedicated to rural development and community welfare.

We focus on infrastructure development, sanitation, water management, education, and sustainable agriculture to improve the quality of life for all residents.
